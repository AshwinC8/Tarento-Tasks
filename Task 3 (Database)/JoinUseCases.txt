Few useCases of Database Joins are :

1. Healthcare Information Systems:
Real-time healthcare applications often deal with diverse sources of patient data, such as electronic health records, medical imaging, and laboratory results.
By using joins, the application can correlate information from multiple data tables to provide a comprehensive patient profile for medical professionals.
This unified view can assist doctors in making timely and accurate decisions, especially in critical situations.

2. Online Gaming Platforms:
Real-time online gaming applications often require integrating data from different gaming servers, user profiles, and in-game purchases. 
Joins help combine these datasets to provide a seamless gaming experience. 
For instance, when a player logs in, the application can retrieve their gaming history, friends' activity, and in-game items through joins to present a personalized and engaging interface.
